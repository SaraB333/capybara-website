Fun Facts!

Did You Know?

The Capybara is the largest rodent on earth!

They’re closely related to Guinea Pigs and Rock Cavys, and more distantly related to Chinchillas and Agouti!

What About This?

They Eat Their Own Poop?

They do so in the morning!

That’s when their poo is protein rich from the high number of microbes digesting the previous day’s meals.

Because the grasses they eat are so hard to digest, eating their waste essentially allows them to digest it twice.
